Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my club login?

Please use the 'Forgot Password' link on the Club Portal login page or contact platform support.

I didn't receive my QR code after registering.

Please check your spam/junk folder first. If it's not there, try logging into your event dashboard (if available) or contact the event organizer club.

Can I cancel my event registration?

Cancellation policies depend on the event organizer. Please contact the club directly. There might be a cancellation option available on the event details page before a certain date.
